Welcome to Habitique

Born out of a passion for interior design and a belief that every home should tell its own unique story, Habitique was founded to offer not only a curated collection of furniture and decor, but also a creative playground where ideas flourish.

Our Story

Established in 2020, Habitique began as a modest vision in a bustling cityscape—much like a hidden gem waiting to be discovered. Inspired by the belief that possessions and art have the power to make a house feel like home, we embarked on a journey to curate future heirlooms and bespoke pieces.

Each piece in our collection carries a story, drawing on diverse influences and our founder's passion for foraging the world for inspiration.

Our Vision

At Habitique, we envision a world where every space is transformed into a sanctuary of creativity and comfort. Our goal is to pioneer sustainable design practices and innovative collaborations that not only elevate interiors but also inspire lasting connections. We're here to reimagine home, one future heirloom at a time.

Our Craftsmanship

At Habitique, our commitment to craftsmanship is as unwavering as our creative spirit. Every piece of furniture and decor is designed in-house and crafted by skilled artisans right here in Nigeria.

We use sustainably sourced materials and time-honored techniques to ensure that each creation isn't just beautiful—it's built to last.
